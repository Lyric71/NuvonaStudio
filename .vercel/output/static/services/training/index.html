03 Full-day · Includes live campaign audit

LinkedIn Ads Deep Dive

LinkedIn Ads is the most precise B2B targeting tool available. Most campaigns waste that precision.

LinkedIn commands 41% of total B2B ad budgets and delivers 121% ROAS. But at CPCs of €5 to €9 and CPMs of €20 to €35, a poorly structured campaign burns budget fast. The most common failures are structural, not creative.
